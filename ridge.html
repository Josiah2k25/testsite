About Feather Ridge

Welcome to Feather Ridge, a pristine residential community nestled in the rolling hills of Edinburg. Our carefully planned neighborhood offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and modern convenience, creating an idyllic setting for your dream home.

Feather Ridge is designed with families in mind, featuring spacious lots, thoughtfully designed green spaces, and premium amenities that foster a sense of community and enhance your quality of life.
